#Economy info
@red@Economy Info:
@blu@Main currency?
@yel@On Zamron we use the 1b coins as currency, Coins are
@yel@worthless. You can gain 1b coins from all
@yel@NPC's and you randomly get them from events too!

@blu@Where do I spend my 1b coins?
@yel@On Zamron you can spend your 1b coins freely at every
@yel@shop at ::shops. You can't use 1b coin on shops that
@yel@use a point system as currency, these shops will tell
@yel@you when it uses a point system as currency!
